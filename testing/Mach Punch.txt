'''Mach Punch''' (Japanese: '''гғһгғғгғҸгғ‘гғігғҒ''' ''Mach Punch'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Fighting}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation II]]. Prior to [[Generation III]], it was the [[signature move]] of {{p|Hitmonchan}}, but {{p|Tyrogue}} could still inherit it via {{pkmn|breeding}}.

==Effect==
Mach Punch inflicts damage. It has a [[priority]] of +1, so it is used before all moves that do not have increased priority.
