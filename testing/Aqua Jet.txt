'''Aqua Jet'''  (Japanese: '''アクアジェット''' ''Aqua Jet'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Water}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation IV]].

==Effect==
Aqua Jet inflicts damage. It has a [[priority]] of +1, so is used before all moves that do not have increased priority.

===Generation VIII===
====Pokémon Legends: Arceus====
Aqua Jet raises the user's action speed.

====Pokémon Legends: Z-A====
Like all other moves that previously possessed priority, Aqua Jet causes the user to dash forward to make damaging contact with the target (although terrain may interfere with doing so).
