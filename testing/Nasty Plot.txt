It is:
* [[TR68]] in [[Pok√©mon Sword and Shield]].
* [[TM063|TM63]] in [[Pok√©mon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl]].

==Effect==
Nasty Plot raises the user's {{stat|Special Attack}} stat by two stages.

Nasty Plot is a part of [[Contest combination|Contest Spectacular combination]] and using it will cause {{m|Baton Pass}} and {{m|Stored Power}} to give an extra three appeal points if used in the next turn.

If powered up by a [[Darkinium Z]] into Z-Nasty Plot, all of the user's lowered stats are reset.
