'''Ice Beam''' (Japanese: '''れいとうビーム''' ''Freezing Beam'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Ice}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation I]].

It is:
* [[TM13]] in [[Generation I]], from [[Generation III]] to {{g|Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon}}, in {{g|Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl}}, as well as in [[Pokémon Legends: Z-A]].
* [[TM55]] in [[Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee!]].
* [[TR05]] in {{g|Sword and Shield}}.
* [[TM135]] in {{g|Scarlet and Violet}}.

==Effect==
===Generation I to V===
Ice Beam inflicts damage and has a 10% chance of {{status|Freeze|freezing}} the target.

===Generation VI onwards===
Ice Beam's power was lowered from 95 to 90.

==Trivia==
* Though Ice Beam freezes its targets very often in the {{pkmn|animated series}}, it has a very low chance of freezing the opponent in the {{pkmn|games}} (10%).
* Every {{type|Ice}} Pokémon can learn this move except for {{p|Snom}}, {{p|Chien-Pao}}, and {{p|Rotom|Frost Rotom}}.
