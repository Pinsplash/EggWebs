'''Grass Knot''' (Japanese: '''„Åè„Åï„ÇÄ„Åô„Å≥''' ''Grass Knot'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Grass}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation IV]] that deals varying damage.

It is:
* [[TM86]] from Generation IV to {{g|Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon}}, and in {{g|Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl}}.
* [[TR77]] in [[Pok√©mon Sword and Shield]].
* [[TM081]] in {{g|Scarlet and Violet}}.

It may be a counterpart to the move {{m|Low Kick}}.

==Effect==
