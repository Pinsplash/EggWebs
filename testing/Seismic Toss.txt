==Effect==
Seismic Toss inflicts damage equal to the user's [[level]].

===Generation I===
Although Seismic Toss is a Fighting-type move, its type does not alter the amount of damage it inflicts. Seismic Toss is not affected by type immunities, so it can hit {{type|Ghost}} Pokأ©mon.

===Generation II onwards===
Seismic Toss is now affected by type immunities; therefore, it cannot usually affect Ghost-type Pokأ©mon.

Seismic Toss can be used as the second move of a [[Contest combination|Pokأ©mon Contest combination]], with the user gaining 2 bonus appeal points if {{m|Fake Out}} was used in the previous turn. It can also form part of a Contest Spectacular combination, with the user gaining an extra three appeal points if any of the moves {{m|Entrainment}}, {{m|Hold Hands}}, or {{m|Play Nice}} was used in the prior turn.

From [[Pokأ©mon Super Mystery Dungeon]] on, Seismic Toss is a status move and does not deal direct damage. It will instead throw its target at another Pokأ©mon (either of which can be a teammate or enemy) and can also throw an enemy Pokأ©mon at a revealed [[Dungeon tile#Effect tile|trap]]. If another Pokأ©mon is struck, they will both take 10 damage and the thrown Pokأ©mon will prioritize landing behind them. The move has a 20-tile frontal cone range from the user's position; if the target cannot be thrown anywhere, they will land one tile behind their position (or on an adjacent tile or the same tile if obstructed), which can trigger a hidden trap.
