'''Guard Split''' (Japanese: '''ガードシェア''' ''Guard Share'') is a non-damaging {{type|Psychic}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation V]].

==Effect==
Guard Split averages the user's {{stat|Defense}} and {{stat|Special Defense}} stats with those of the target Pokémon. Both the user's and the target's stat changes are ignored when calculating the average.

Guard Split bypasses {{stat|accuracy}} checks to always hit, unless the target is in the [[semi-invulnerable turn]] of a move such as {{m|Dig}} or {{m|Fly}}.

Stats changes, as well as [[Assault Vest]], [[Eviolite]] and other Defense/Special Defense increasing effects, all use the Pokémon’s altered stats rather than its original stats in calculation.

Once switched out, the Pokémon’s altered stats return to normal.

When used on or by a Pokémon with altered stats, Guard Split uses the Pokémon’s altered stats rather than the original to calculate the new average.

If powered up by a [[Psychium Z]] into Z-Guard Split, the user's {{stat|Speed}} stat is raised by one stage.

Guard Split will fail if used in a [[Tera Raid Battle]] (on Boss and allies alike).
