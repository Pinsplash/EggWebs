'''Body Slam''' (Japanese: '''„ÅÆ„Åó„Åã„Åã„Çä''' ''Lean On'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Normal}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation I]].

It is:
* [[TM08]] in Generation I
* [[TR01]] in {{g|Sword and Shield}}.
* [[TM066]] in {{g|Scarlet and Violet}}.
* [[TM033]] in [[Pok√©mon Legends: Z-A]].

==Effect==
Body Slam inflicts damage and has a 30% chance of {{status|Paralysis|paralyzing}} the target. 

===Generation I===
Body Slam cannot paralyze {{type|Normal}} Pok√©mon.

===Generations II to V===
Body Slam can now paralyze Normal-type Pok√©mon.

===Generation VI onward===
Body Slam can no longer paralyze {{type|Electric}} Pok√©mon, due to them now being immune to {{status|paralysis}}.

If the target has the {{m|Minimize}}d status, Body Slam now deals double its regular damage and bypasses {{stat|accuracy}} checks to always hit, unless the target is in the [[semi-invulnerable turn]] of a move such as {{m|Dig}} or {{m|Fly}}.
