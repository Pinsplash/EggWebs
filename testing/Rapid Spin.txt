'''Rapid Spin''' (Japanese: '''こうそくスピン''' ''Rapid Spin'') is a damage-dealing {{type|Normal}} [[move]] introduced in [[Generation II]].

==Effect==
===Generations II-VII===
Rapid Spin inflicts damage and then removes the effects of any [[bound|binding effects]] and {{m|Leech Seed}} from the user, as well as [[entry hazard]]s from the user's side of the field (unless the user has fainted as a result of the move, such as from {{a|Rough Skin}}).

Rapid Spin has a power of 20.

===Generation VIII onwards===
Rapid Spin now has a power of 50. When inflicting damage, Rapid Spin now also raises the user's {{stat|Speed}} by one stage.

==={{g|UNITE}}===
In Pokémon UNITE, Rapid Spin is {{p|Blastoise}}'s and {{p|Tsareena}}'s second move.

Blastoise can obtain the move by reaching level 7 as {{p|Wartortle}} and upgrading {{m|Skull Bash}} into it instead of {{m|Surf}}. The user withdraws into its shell and begins rapidly spinning, resetting the cooldown of its other move. The user is unstoppable for the duration of Rapid Spin and can freely move about the battlefield. While spinning, the user will damage and slow nearby enemies. The user's next basic attack after Rapid Spin ends becomes boosted. For the duration of Rapid Spin, the mechanics of the user's Basic attacks as well as Hydro Pump and Water Spout are changed. At level 13 or higher, the user gains a massive amount of Defense and Sp. Defense for the duration of Rapid Spin.

Tsareena starts with the move as {{p|Bounsweet}}. The user spins in the designated direction, dealing damage to any enemy hit. When this move hits, the user's movement speed is increased for a short time. Upon evolving from {{p|Steenee}} at level 6, it is replaced by either {{m|Trop Kick}} or {{m|Grassy Glide}}.

==Trivia==
* Rapid Spin was one of the first [[Generation II]] moves introduced and named. It was seen being used by {{OBP|Mewtwo|M01}}'s {{p|Blastoise|Blastoisetwo}} in ''[[M01|Mewtwo Strikes Back]]'', prior to the release of {{2v2|Gold|Silver}}.
* The animation for Rapid Spin when used by {{p|Blastoise}} in {{g|UNITE}} and [[Pokkén Tournament DX]] is akin to how [[Ash's Squirtle]] uses {{m|Hydro Pump}} in {{aniseries|PTS}}, which appears to be a [[List of references to popular culture in Pokémon|reference]] to {{wp|Gamera}}'s spinning flight.
